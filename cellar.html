You descend into the darkness. Rats scurry up the stairs by your feet. A moist, rotting smell greets your nostrils.

You come to the end of a tunnel. Water drips from the ceiling and gathers in sludge around your feet.

Rough-hewn wooden boards, marked with arcane symbols, block your path.

Try to break down the boards.

Go back up the stairs and into the house.

Go back up and knock on the door.
