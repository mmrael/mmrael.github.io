About Me

Madeline Rael is a graphic designer based in Brooklyn, NY. She received her B.A. in Media and Communication Studies at UNC-Chapel Hill and is pursuing an A.A.S. in Communication Design at Parsons School of Design.

Relishing in a love for stories, Madeline has designed two non-fiction books about the Golden Age of Hollywood, covers for new editions of HAMLET and the ARABIAN NIGHTS, and an original concept for a children’s picture book. She is in the process of designing a periodical about Hollywood film. Designing across web and print, she also holds expertise in branding and packaging.

Madeline imbues her loves of history, film, and visual storytelling in each project to which she tends. In and outside of her creative practice, she values authenticity, curiosity, and whimsy.
