You avoid the shadows and run past the statue hiding in the darkness...

...and come to a bladed door at the end of the corridor.

You go through the door, slamming it behind you.

You are now in a vast room with stained glass windows and huge pipe organ in the corner.

A towering, hooded figure stands before you. He cackles and screeches:

"Oh, you poor fool... this is the last time you will breach my house... Come, my pets, attack!"

RUN AWAY!

Try to bargain with the hooded figure.

Shine your torch in his face.
