Cookies & Cream Brownie Cheesecake Bars

These cookies & cream brownie cheesecake bars are the ultimate dessert mashup. With a rich chocolatey brownie base, creamy cheesecake filling, and chunks of crunchy cookies, these bars are an indulgent treat for any sweet tooth. Perfect for sharing with friends and family.

-- Alvin Zhou at Tasty





Ingredients

  • 1 box brownie mix
  • 24 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 36 chocolate sandwich cookies

Preparation

  1. Prepare the brownie mix in a bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix together the cream cheese and the sugar until there are no clumps.
  3. Add the egg and vanilla, stirring until evenly combined.
  4. Spread the brownie batter in an even layer at the bottom of the pan.
  5. Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C) and grease a 9x13 (23x33 cm) baking pan.
  6. Place 24 of the sandwich cookies in a single layer on top of the brownie batter. To fill in the edges, cut 6 of the cookies in half and nudge those in.
  7. Pour the cheesecake batter on top, spreading it evenly over the cookies.
  8. Crush the remaining 6 cookies and sprinkle on top of the cheesecake batter.
  9. Bake for about 45 minutes, until the edges are slightly golden brown and the middle is slightly jiggly.
  10. Cool and chill for at least 2 hours.
  11. Slice and serve.
  12. Enjoy!
